ABOUT US

Sam Electrocomm Pty. Ltd. T/A Electronics Repair Hub, has been working in the field of electronics, security solutions & telecommunications repair since 2011.

Sub-contractors for a major telecommunications network, we have been providing installation and repair services for the NBN. Servicing the Southern and South Eastern suburbs of Melbourne, we have also been repairing electronics and selling & installing CCTV security systems for many years.

Our highly experienced and expert technicians have over 25 years experience in repairing electronics. They have worked for a number of years in the repair and service departments of international companies like Wipro (India) & Fujitsu (Australia).

Why Choose Us

  • Customer satisfaction is our primary goal.
  • Qualified and professionally trained technicians with over 25 years of experience in electronics repair.
  • Honest opinion about whether it is economically justified to repair an item will be given.
  • Faulty parts that have been replaced can be collected with the repaired equipment.
