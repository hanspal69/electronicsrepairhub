SURVEILLANCE EQUIPMENT

SALES & INSTALLATIONS OF CAMERAS & RECORDERS

We sell and install Dahua security cameras & recorders. Avialable in 4MP, 5MP, 6MP & 8MP resolution with fixed focal lengths or variable (motorised) focal lengths.

Two network formats are avaialble: IP (on Ethernet) or HDCVI (high definition composite video interface on coaxial).

Cameras can have IR infrared light source for use at night and the number in brackets next is the range of the camera at night.

Starlight Cameras are cameras which have special sensors, which provide improved night vision in low light conditions. Please tap or click on a card below to find out more about the type of cameras that we offer.
